What this site collects

Browsing this site requires no account and no personal information. The eligibility checker and savings estimator run entirely in your browser; the answers and figures you enter are not transmitted to this site or to anyone else, and are not stored.

Lead and quote forms

Where this site offers a quote-request or comparison form, the information you submit (such as name, email, phone number, county, and the type of work or coverage you are asking about) is shared with the service providers who respond to that request, as described next to the form. These forms are submitted only when you choose to submit them, and any consent to be contacted is collected by an unchecked checkbox at the point of submission.

Third-party embeds

Insurance comparison forms on this site may be operated by third-party lead platforms. Information entered into an embedded third-party form is governed by that operator's privacy policy, which is linked at the form.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not set cookies of its own. If privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics are added, this policy will be updated first.
