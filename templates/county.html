What the grant is

Two parts: a free program-assigned wind mitigation inspection, then a matching grant for improvements that inspection recommends. The state pays $2 for every $1 the homeowner spends, up to {{ msfh.grant_terms.max_grant_usd | usd }}. {{ msfh.grant_terms.low_income_exception }}. Reimbursement comes after completed, inspected work; it is not a loan.

Basic eligibility: {{ msfh.eligibility.property | lower }}, built before 2008, with insurance Coverage A at or below {{ msfh.eligibility.insured_value_cap_usd | usd }}, and a valid homeowners insurance policy. A program inspection must be completed before any grant is issued.

Apply and confirm details at mysafeflhome.com, administered by the {{ msfh.administrator }}.

SEC. 03 / INSURANCE SAVINGS

How the discounts work on a {{ county.name }} premium

Mitigation discounts do not apply to your whole premium. They apply to the windstorm portion, which in Florida typically runs {{ statewide.inspection.windstorm_share_of_premium_pct[0] }} to {{ statewide.inspection.windstorm_share_of_premium_pct[1] }} percent of the total bill. Documented features commonly reduce that portion by {{ statewide.inspection.discount_range_on_windstorm_portion_pct[0] }} to {{ statewide.inspection.discount_range_on_windstorm_portion_pct[1] }} percent. Ranges vary by home, county, and carrier, and savings are never guaranteed.
