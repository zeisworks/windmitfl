SEC. 01 / HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS

Three moving parts, one paper trail

Florida law requires residential insurers to discount the windstorm portion of your premium when your home has documented wind-resistant features. The document that proves those features is a wind mitigation inspection report on form {{ statewide.form.id }}. And for low- and moderate-income homeowners, the My Safe Florida Home program pays for the inspection and matches money spent on the upgrades the inspection recommends.

That is the whole system: an inspection that documents features, discounts the law requires carriers to apply, and a grant program that helps pay for hardening. Every page on this site is one of those three parts, broken down by county, with a "last verified" date and primary sources.

This site is independent. It is not run by an inspector, a contractor, or an insurer, and it does not tell you what to buy. It shows the numbers and links the primary sources: the Office of Insurance Regulation, the My Safe Florida Home portal, and the Florida Statutes.
