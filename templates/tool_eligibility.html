My Safe Florida Home eligibility checker

Six questions, answered locally in your browser. Nothing is submitted or stored. This is an informational screening against the published program rules; the Florida Department of Financial Services makes every actual determination at mysafeflhome.com.

1. Is the home a site-built, single-family house that you own and occupy as your primary residence, with a homestead exemption?
2. Was the home built before 2008?
3. Do you carry a valid homeowners insurance policy on the home?
4. Is your dwelling coverage (Coverage A) {{ el.insured_value_cap_usd | usd }} or less?
5. How does your household income compare with your county's median income? Verified dollar thresholds are published on each county page; HUD income limits are the underlying source.
6. Is any applicant homeowner age 60 or older?
